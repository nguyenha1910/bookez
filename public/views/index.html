Our Story

We are two undergraduate students who started Book-EZ as part of the Entrepreneurship & Innovation Program at Clark University. The idea came from our struggle to find affordable textbooks required for classes. With a passion to solve this real-life problem facing by many real-life college students, our mission for Book-EZ is to make books more affordable and available for the Clark community.
